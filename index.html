Apex International School

THE SCHOOL WITH INTERNATIONAL
STANDARD

Apex International school is a secondary school affiliated to CBSE, Delhi, established in the year 2005. It is situated at Odumbra, in Olavanna Panchayath, Calicut. The school is located in a sprawling 3-acre green campus, 1.5 km from mini bye-pass. It is a co-educational institution that caters to students from K.G to Std X. The mission of the school is to Promote Education that builds fundamental characters such as honesty, compassion, courage, persistence, and responsibility. The management envisions a completely different school with personalized care to develop intellectual and social skills. The school is an extension of the family where we bring out the best out of the students through the partnership of parents.
